What is a Cracker?

Among the many terms related to security and computing, we find crackers, a word that defines a certain person who uses all his knowledge in computing to break some security system, either for profit, as a sign of protest, defiance or even to perform industrial espionage.


What are its characteristics?

  • They have a vast knowledge of information security.
  • They know well the area in which they are going to work.
  • They know how to eliminate various defenses in an efficient way.

Cracker types


Pirates

Those who, with their knowledge, break the protection and licensing systems of different software.

Phreakers

Specialists in attacking telephone systems, tracing calls or making calls at no cost.

Insiders

Crackers who work inside a company and attack it from the inside.
