Hacker

Hackers are individuals with excellent computer skills, the ability to create and explore the software and hardware of a computer, with the knowledge to discover vulnerabilities in a target system whose original creators had not anticipated.

Hacker types

The main types of hackers according to the ethical nature of their attacks are:

  • Black Hat
  • White Hat
  • Gray Hat

Black Hat

The unauthorized entry into a computer system for the purpose of causing damage to the data or the system itself. An example is the installation of malware to gain remote access and steal user information or send malicious content to infect other systems.

White Hat

This type of hacking is performed with the authorization and consent of all parties involved to find out the security of a system. It aims to help software developers to improve their security mechanisms and is nowadays a discipline that is well remunerated by security and software development companies.

Gray Hat

It is somewhere between white hat and black hat. It performs different types of attacks in them. Its motivations are not as destructive as a blackhat or as purely professional as a white hat.
